How does Notos work?

Notos provides a faster, reliable transport for you to get to work or even explore the city. Notos has many pick-up and drop-off areas around the city. All areas are near the road and are in busy areas. Every Notos station has signs to indicate it.

Notos bikes also have navigation. You won't get lost and the navigation will show you how long your journey takes.

No more traffic, arrive on time.

How to get a Notos bike

1. Scan

Scan the QR code on the side of the Notos bike.
Follow the instructions of the phone.

2. Pay

Sign up or Log in to your account.
Choose the plan suitable for you.
Pay with your card.

3. Go

After the payment is approved, the Notos bike will be unlocked. Check the screen of the Notos bike. You can find how many minutes are left for your 30-minutes ride.

By clicking on the map, enter your destination and it will navigate you. Voice navigation is included, so that you don't have to look at the screen while riding.

Get on the bike and enjoy your journey with Notos.

4. Park

After your ride, just park the bike at any Notos station. Park the bike at any empty stops in the station.

Click the end button on the screen or the leave button on your phone. The bike will be locked automatically.

Thank you for riding with Notos.

*Please do not park the Notos bike in the street. You one small step, makes a big impact to our city.
