About Notos

Notos is a bike sharing company. We aim to provide a transport for you to get to work or hang out on holidays. With Notos, you don't have to worry about the traffic. Also, you can get some exercise, instead of sitting inside a bus.

Hate getting lost? Notos bike has navigation for you. Our navigation also show how long your journey takes. You will get to your destination on time with Notos.
